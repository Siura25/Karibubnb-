About Us

KaribuBnB is a homegrown Kenyan platform designed to connect travelers with local hosts. Whether you’re looking for a cozy stay or an opportunity to host, we make it easy, affordable, and safe.

Our mission is to make travel in Africa more accessible while supporting communities and giving hosts the opportunity to earn.
